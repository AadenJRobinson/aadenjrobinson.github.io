Career: Computer Science/Computer Engineering

Career: The career I am interested in is computer science or computer engineering. For multiple years now, I have been interested in coding and have put my skills to use in various projects. Coding is my hobby, my one way to get a break from my schoolwork. I think I will enjoy a job in software development/computer science (coding software) because I enjoy it now. I may also enjoy computer engineering (developing ideas for newer and more powerful computers) because it seems interesting to me, as my neighbor has helped me with building my own computer, which seemed interesting to me.

Workload/Demand: The demand of either of these jobs can vary significantly due to fluctuation in the requirements of projects being made. Sometimes hours may be flexible, other times not. 

Salary: Computer science/engineering jobs can vary in pay, from around $90000 up to $200000.

Educational Requirements: Most computer science/engineering careers will require at least a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or computer engineering as well as a good background going into these types of jobs (experience, work ethic, dedication, etc.). In order to get to this level of education, I will have to continue on my path in the Engineering Academy and get into a good college, which will allow me to be a viable candidate for a career in this path. 

Special Benefits: The main special benefit of a computer science job is that it is very flexible, with employees being able to work from home if sick or on a trip, for example. This is possible because most work would be virtual, which allows for it to be done anywhere with internet.
