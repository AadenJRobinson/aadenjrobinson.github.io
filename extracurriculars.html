Extracurriculars

My primary (and only) extracurricular is taekwondo, which I do at the studio in the Lake Elsinore Outlets. This is quite different from most sports because it includes one aspects that is not found in many other sports: respect. All martial arts focus highly on this, conforming to cultural practices, respecting masters, and keeping good grades and behavior outside of class. Taekwondo allows me to build up many physical traits in myself such as strength, endurance, and reaction, while maintaining discipline and another good reason to keep my grades up. I plan on becoming a black belt before the end of my junior year in high school.
