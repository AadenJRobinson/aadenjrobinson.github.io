Extracurriculars

Taekwondo: For over three years now, I have been doing Taekwondo as my sport and physical activity of choice. After working my way up throughout my first three years of high school, I obtained my black belt close to the end of the 2024-25 school year. I am now more involved than ever, helping to teach the 7-12-year-old students. I have competed in 5 Taekwondo tournaments as of now, and hope to enter more in the future when I have the chance.

Academic Decathlon: I have been a part of the TCHS Academic Decathlon since it was introduced to the school in the 2023-24 year. (Academic Decathlon is a high school competition between schools that tests students in seven main subjects, which have their specifics changed year-by-year.) I particularly enjoy doing this program because of the people that I often meet in it that are interested in it as well, along with the practice in testing and public speaking that it gives me (skills that are very important in this time of my life).
