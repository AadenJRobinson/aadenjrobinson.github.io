Service

I have already completed my 40 hours of high school community service, in the form of cleaning and organizing at Temescal Canyon High School as well as helping with various Easter events at Horsethief Canyon Ranch. My main project is at Horsethief Canyon Ranch, where I organize a library of over 300 books to eventually lend to people in the community. This project is not done yet, but I plan on completing it by early 2025. Once organized, this library will be a helpful tool for many people in our community, and also provide community service opportunities to ambitious high school students that come after myself.
